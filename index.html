The principle of accumulation of bookings

The principle of accumulating bookings to reduce the cost of a flight is based on the following principles:

  1. Plan ahead: The earlier you book your flight tickets, the cheaper their cost will usually be.
  2. Using promotions and discounts: Keep an eye on airline promotions and special offers to get discounts on booking tickets.
  3. Accumulation of miles: Participate in the airline's loyalty program and accumulate miles for each flight in order to receive a discount on reservations in the future.
